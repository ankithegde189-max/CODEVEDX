Phishing Types & Threat Vector Analysis

Understanding the anatomy of different phishing variations and access mechanics.

Spear Phishing

Targeted attacks aimed at specific individuals or departments. Attackers perform intelligence gathering (OSINT) to build trust and tailor specific content details.

Whaling

A type of spear phishing targeted specifically at C-level executives. Often masquerades as critical court subpoena orders, executive complaints, or business issues.

Smishing & Vishing

SMS phishing (smishing) and voice call phishing (vishing). Targets are contacted via phone requesting urgent action or containing fake tracking links.

Clone Phishing

Creating an identical copy of a legitimate, previously delivered email (with actual attachments or link domains updated) to intercept communications.
